Pav-Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food consisting of a spicy, mashed vegetable curry (bhaji) served with buttered and toasted bread rolls (pav). The bhaji is made by cooking mashed potatoes, tomatoes, peas, and other vegetables with a blend of aromatic spices. It is garnished with butter, chopped onions, and lemon juice, making it a flavorful and hearty dish enjoyed across India.

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 cup cauliflower
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 teaspoon pav bhaji masala

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes, cauliflower, and peas until soft. Mash them and set aside
  2. Heat butter in a pan, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until onions turn golden
  3. Add tomatoes, capsicum, red chili powder, turmeric, and pav bhaji masala. Cook until tomatoes soften. Add the mashed vegetables, mix well, and simmer with water. Mash further for a smooth texture
  4. Slice pav rolls, apply butter, and toast on a pan with pav bhaji masala until golden
